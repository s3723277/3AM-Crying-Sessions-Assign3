Challenges and Learning


UI Developer:

As we are only first year IT students, we are still learning basic coding languages and unable to create our own mobile application from scratch. With the help of a UI developer, they can help us translate our design concepts and ideas into reality using front end technology. Additionally, their choice in software interfaces should also prioritise our app users and business requirements over technology limitations and constraints which will be extremely beneficial when it comes to dividing costs for our project.


UI designer:

A UI designer is responsible for the designing aspect of the app. Not only do they ensure that the app has pleasing aesthetics, but also makes sure that the app corresponds to usability principles, giving users the most enjoyability when using the app.


Software engineer:

Crucial members to our development team as they apply engineering principles to every stage of our mobile application development process, including analysis to the software process and creating a personalised systems for the team on our unique project idea.


Usability researcher:

A usability researcher ensures that the app is to standard with user testing. They hold surveys, test, and gather information about the good and bad about the application. Using this information, they are able to improve the app.
