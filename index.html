Who We Are:


This website is designed to help individuals learn how to use modern day apps and resources.



In this website, you can learn how to use basic functions for apps such as; Facebook, Outlook, WhatsApp, Google Maps and Centrelink.



Our aim is to ensure that the people that struggle to use technology come out of there experience with using Boomer Life Support feeling like they can now tackle their previous issues with absolute ease!
